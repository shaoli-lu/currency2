# 🌍 World Currency (世界货币)

Elevate your global perspective with the World Currency & Crypto Hub—a premium, real-time dashboard designed for travelers, investors, and curious minds. Experience a sleek, bilingually supported (English & Chinese) interface that brings global economic pulse right to your fingertips.

---

## ✨ Stellar Description

The World Currency & Crypto Hub is more than just a converter; it's a window into the global market. Built with a stunning **Glassmorphism design**, it transforms complex financial data into a beautiful, interactive experience. Whether you’re tracking the latest strength of the Euro, searching for the value of the Afghan Afghani, or monitoring the 24-hour volatility of Bitcoin, this app provides a seamless and vibrant journey through the world of finance.

### Why You'll Love It:
*   **🌐 Comprehensive Global Reach**: Support for over 170+ fiat currencies and the top 250 cryptocurrencies.
*   **⚡ Real-Time Intelligence**: Live data fetching from exchange rate APIs and CoinGecko ensures you're always up-to-date.
*   **📱 Immersive Modes**: From an automated **Slideshow** for passive tracking to detailed **Market Lists** for deep dives.
*   **🎨 Premium Aesthetics**: Dynamic gradients, subtle micro-animations, and interactive confetti that makes monitoring markets actually *fun*.
*   **🔍 Smart Discovery**: Instant search allows you to find any currency or coin by name or symbol in a heartbeat.

## 🛠️ Brief Instructions

Getting started is as simple as a tap. Here's how to navigate your new hub:

1.  **Select Your View**: Use the navigation bar at the top to toggle between:
    *   **Slide Show**: A hands-free, rotating display of major global currencies.
    *   **Major Currencies**: A sorted list of the world's most influential trade currencies.
    *   **Low Value**: Discover currencies with the highest exchange rates relative to the USD.
    *   **Crypto**: Dive into the digital frontier with live prices, market caps, and percentage changes.
2.  **Search with Ease**: Use the glass-morphed search bar at the top to filter any list. Just type a country name, currency code (e.g., "EUR"), or coin symbol (e.g., "BTC").
3.  **Control the Flow**: In **Slide Show** mode, click anywhere on the panel to pause/play the rotation.
4.  **Stay Informed**: Check the "As of" timestamp under the header to confirm the freshness of your data.
5.  **Enjoy the Interaction**: Click anywhere on the background to celebrate market moves with a burst of **interactive confetti**! 🎉
